What is AI FITNESS MONITORING DEVICE?

AI Fitness Monitoring Device is a compact, user-friendly system that measures heart rate, blood oxygen (SpO‚ÇÇ), and temperature using reliable sensors and AI-assisted signal processing. It displays clear, real-time readings on an LCD and provides alerts and guidance for quick, at-home health checks.

How it works

The device captures raw signals from onboard sensors, applies filtering and smoothing to reduce noise, and calculates vital metrics like heart rate and SpO‚ÇÇ. A program coded for an Arduino microcontroller handles sampling, processing, and updates the LCD in real time.

How to use it

  1. Press the power button located on the back of the device to turn it on.
  2. After powering on, the LCD will display a welcome message and cycle through introductory slides.
  3. When the LCD shows "Place Finger", put your finger or thumb into the right hole of the sensor box (HR & SpO‚ÇÇ sensor). Wait a few seconds for the heart rate and SpO‚ÇÇ readings to appear.
  4. Next, when the LCD prompts "Place finger/thumb on the pulse", place your finger or thumb into the left hole (pulse analog sensor). Wait a few seconds for the pulse reading to appear.
  5. Then, when the LCD shows "Hold the temp", hold the steel-colored temperature sensor against your hand or the surface you want to measure; the device will show the measured temperature and its changes.

When the measurement sequence completes, the device will display a thank-you message. To run the sequence again, long-press the power button to turn the device off, then press it again to restart and follow the on-screen instructions.
